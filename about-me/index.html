    Publication

    • Unlocking Model Insights: A Dataset for Automated Model Card Generation


      Arxiv; Sept 22, 2023
      https://arxiv.org/abs/2309.12616
      We present a dataset of 500 QA pairs for 25 ML models to train LMs in automating model card generation, highlighting current limitations in understanding and accuracy with ChatGPT3.5, LLaMa, and Galactica.

    • Investigating REV-ERBβ Binding Pocket Dynamics with Implications for Rational Design of Small Molecule Modulators


      PLOS Computational Biology; Jun 14, 2024
      PCOMPBIOLD2401004
      Reference No. NCOMMS2422906
      REVERB α (NR1D1) and REVERB β (NR1D2) are nuclear receptors regulating genes involved in circadian rhythm and metabolism by binding to DNA response elements. This study utilized molecular dynamics and docking studies to analyze the conformational flexibility of REV ERB β and its interactions with small molecule ligands.
